menemen recipe

Menemen is a traditional Turkish egg dish. It is made with eggs, tomatoes, and green peppers. It is cooked in a pan and eaten hot, usually with bread.

pasta recipe

Pasta is a popular dish around the world. It is made with pasta, sauce, and sometimes cheese or meat. It is boiled and served hot, often with grated cheese.

poğaça recipe

Poğaça is a traditional Turkish pastry. It is made with flour, butter, yeast, and cheese or potatoes. It is baked in the oven and usually eaten for breakfast or as a snack.
